About Bhrikuti Hospital

Bhrikuti Hospital Pvt. Ltd. is a modern multi-specialty healthcare facility located in Ward No. 5, Kamal, Jhapa. We are committed to providing quality, affordable, and patient-centered medical services to communities across eastern Nepal, including Jhapa, Morang, Ilam, and Panchthar.

Established on 2082-08-22 B.S. and fully operational since 2082-01-01 B.S., the hospital is equipped with modern facilities and advanced diagnostic services. We specialize in essential healthcare needs, including road traffic injury management, ensuring timely and reliable treatment.

Led by Chairman Mr. Buddhiman Thapa and Executive Director/CEO Mr. Pratap Karki, our dedicated team strives to deliver compassionate and efficient care you can trust.

Our Vision

We envision a society where no one loses their life to curable diseases or accidents, ensuring accessible, high-quality care for all.

Our Mission

Bhrikuti Hospital commits to providing world-class diagnostic and treatment facilities, performing biomedical research, and offering affordable healthcare services.

Core Objectives

  • To operate featuring one minor OT and six advanced modular operating theaters.
  • Specialize in road traffic injury (RTI) management to save lives.
  • Maintain the highest standards of cleanliness, safety, and operational excellence.

Comprehensive Services

We offer comprehensive care across OPD, emergency (24/7), ICU, medicine, ortho/surgery wards, physiotherapy, USG/X-ray diagnostics, endoscopy, laparoscopy, pediatric, gynecology, and more.
