About Bhrikuti Hospital

Bhrikuti Hospital Pvt. Ltd. is a leading multi-specialty healthcare facility dedicated to delivering quality medical services at affordable rates in the eastern region of Nepal.

Located in Ward No. 5, Kamal, Jhapa, our hospital addresses critical needs like road traffic injury management, and advanced diagnostics for surrounding districts including Jhapa, Morang, Ilam, Panchthar and others.

B for Best • H for Healthcare • P for Patient • L for Life

Our Vision

We envision a society where no one loses their life to curable diseases or accidents, ensuring accessible, high-quality care for all.

Our Mission

Bhrikuti Hospital commits to providing world-class diagnostic and treatment facilities, performing biomedical research, and offering affordable healthcare services.

Core Objectives

  • To operate featuring one minor OT and six advanced modular operating theaters.
  • Specialize in road traffic injury (RTI) management to save lives.
  • Maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and safety.

Services Overview

We offer comprehensive care across OPD, emergency (24/7), ICU, medicine, ortho/surgery wards, physiotherapy, USG/X-ray diagnostics, endoscopy, laparoscopy, pediatric, gynecology and more.
